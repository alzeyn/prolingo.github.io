About App

What problems we solve?

There is inefficiency in language teaching, especially academic and specialized ones, and a lack of platform for combining language learning with vocational skills. Our mission is to make learning academic languages and professional skills accessible, convenient and effective for everyone, creating a community where everyone can grow and achieve their academic and career goals.

Start

How to start using the application?

First, identify your native language, then select the language you would like to learn, and finally select your professional field. This is necessary to create personalized courses and content that suit your exact needs. Choosing your professional field will help you integrate specialized topics and terminology into your training materials, making the learning process more relevant and effective for your goals. It will also make it easier to find a language exchange partner, making conversations more meaningful and beneficial to your professional growth.
